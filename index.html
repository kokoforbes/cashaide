About

CashAide Has Two Main
Features That Helps Make
Handling Cash Simple

Collect &
Give Cash
give & save
change

CashAide connects merchants/traders who have excess cash from sales, to customers who needs to have little cash at hand. This saves merchants/traders the stress of going to the bank to deposit cash and saves the customers the stress of looking for an ATM and joining the long queues. Customers can put up a request and be directed to merchants within walking distance to get cash.
